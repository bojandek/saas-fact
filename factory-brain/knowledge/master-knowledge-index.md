## 💡 Key Insights (Across All Files)

### Universal Principles
1. **Measurement precedes improvement**: SLI → SLO → action
2. **Smaller deployments reduce risk**: Deploy frequently, not rarely
3. **Culture > tools**: Blameless postmortems matter more than Splunk
4. **Autonomy at scale**: Trust with boundaries works
5. **Optimize for stage**: Different stages need different metrics
6. **Consistency beats perfection**: Finish good, not perfect
7. **Compose patterns**: No silver bullet, combine approaches
8. **User experience is everything**: From design to load time

### Anti-Patterns to Avoid (Across All Files)
- ❌ "Move fast and break things" without team structure
- ❌ Shipping without observability
- ❌ Measuring everything (track 5-10 key metrics)
- ❌ Organizational structure disconnected from product
- ❌ Perfectionism over shipping
- ❌ Process over people
- ❌ Central deployment authority (slow shipping)
- ❌ Ignoring cohort analysis (aggregate metrics lie)
